Hierarchical Topic Detection

1) Primary Test System Description

We make use of a combination of k-means clustering 
and divisive centroid clustering approach for 
hierarchical topic detection. 
K-means clustering is used to group the stories 
to general topic. 
Each general topic is divided to specific ones 
with the help of divisive centroid clustering.  

2) Training

We do not conduct any training process.
